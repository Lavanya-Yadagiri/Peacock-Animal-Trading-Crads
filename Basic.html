PEACOCK

Male peacocks are famous for their stunning tail feathers, which they display in courtship rituals.

  • Scientific Name: Pavo cristatus
  • Average Length: 100-120 centimeters (body), up to 2 meters including tail
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: Forests, farmlands, and gardens

Peacocks are renowned for their vibrant, iridescent tail feathers, often called a "train," which feature eye-like patterns in hues of blue, green, and gold. These birds are native to South Asia but can be found in various regions due to domestication and cultural significance.
